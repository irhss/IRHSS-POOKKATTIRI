About Our School

IRHSS Pookkattiri is a recognized higher secondary school in Malappuram district, Kerala. Established in 1977, the school is committed to academic excellence and the holistic development of students. We provide a supportive learning environment that nurtures knowledge, discipline, and strong moral values.
